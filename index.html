Conduit

What is conduit?

Conduit is a free app for iOS that lets you get many utilities deployed onto your ios device. Conduit is free, and will remain free forever. Conduit is an alternative for jailbreak, but it is not associated with jailbreak or its utilities in any way.

Why is it free?

Well, i do not have any worries for money at this time, and since conduit is hosted on github pages, there is no payments involved in hosting conduit.
