Conduit

What is conduit?

Conduit is a free app for iOS and Android that lets you get many utilities deployed onto your ios device. Conduit is free, and will remain free forever. Conduit is a sort of personal, interactive weblog that provides both the reader and the writer access to many interactive components.

Why is it free?

Well, i do not have any worries for money at this time, and since conduit is hosted on github pages, there is no payments involved in hosting conduit.

Sounds great! how do i install it?

Just click the download button below to start the installation process. If you can't see the download button, it means that your device is not compatible with your conduit.
